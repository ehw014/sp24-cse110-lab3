Agenda: How to improve our business.
Old Business: Our competitors and challenges to success.
New Business: Things we should buy, customers' taste, and any other ideas to improve sales.

Last meeting we discussed many of the issues plaguing our business. We lack appliances such as a hand mixer or oven to make muffins.
Some of the flavors do not appeal to our youthful customers. Also, muffins can be dry so customers often can't finish a whole muffin without coughing and choking.

Despite the complaints of some people, we have created a list of issues we must address:

  1. Buy an oven
  2. Offer beverages
  3. No one likes poppyseed muffins
